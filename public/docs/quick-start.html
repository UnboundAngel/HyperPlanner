Checklist

  • Open the app and switch to Demo if you just want to explore.
  • Create a task: “handoff tomorrow 3pm @work !high”.
  • Drag it onto the calendar, then move it between board lanes.
  • Toggle layout presets (Minimal, Dashboard, Kanban) and save your favorite.
  • Glance at Theme Lab tokens; editing unlocks after sign-up.

Next steps
