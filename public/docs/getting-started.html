Getting Started

First 10 minutes with HyperPlanner.

Capture, schedule, and finish your first tasks.

← Back to landing

Setup

  • Pick a layout preset that fits your screen (Minimal for focus, Dashboard for overview).
  • Add 3 tasks with contexts (@work/@personal) and priorities (!high/!low).
  • Schedule at least one task with start/end times to feel the calendar flow.
  • Turn on reminders and set your daily summary hour.

Daily rhythm

  • Use Quick Capture for new items; drag to calendar or board to commit.
  • Snooze or reschedule from the task modal to keep today realistic.
  • Review “Next Up” and the focus chip each morning.
  • Need help? noreply@hyperplanner.today.
