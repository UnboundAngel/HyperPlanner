How to reach us

  • Email: noreply@hyperplanner.today
  • Describe your issue with steps, browser, and screenshots.
  • Include whether you’re in Demo or signed-in mode.
  • Attach template JSON if the issue involves templates.

Response times

  • We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.
  • Critical outages: include “urgent” in subject.
  • Feature ideas: we log and prioritize by demand.
  • Security issues: please disclose privately.
