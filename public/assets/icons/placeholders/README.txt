Temporary placeholder icons for Phase 3 (iconography) work.

Files here are simple SVG stubs so the UI can be wired without final art. Each filename is the semantic identifier we expect a real icon pack to include. When generating/replacing, match the role and keep the transparent background.

Suggested prompts for final assets (flat, 2-tone, outline-friendly):
- work.svg – laptop/briefcase icon, calm neutral stroke, minimal detail.
- personal.svg – home/heart combo, soft corners, friendly fill.
- study.svg – book + checkmark, crisp lines, legible at 16px.
- health.svg – heart + pulse line, balanced stroke weight.
- travel.svg – paper plane or luggage tag, dynamic angle.
- finance.svg – card + spark/dot, confident outline.
- automation.svg – gear + spark, modern minimal.
- alert.svg – bell with dot badge, high-contrast silhouette.
- calendar.svg – calendar grid with a highlighted cell.
- tasks.svg – checklist with two ticks, simple outline.

Replace these SVGs with real assets or swap to PNG if needed; keep filenames for predictable mapping in the UI.
