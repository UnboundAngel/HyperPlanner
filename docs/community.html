Community Guidelines

To keep our community helpful and welcoming, please follow these guidelines:

Be Respectful

Treat others as you'd like to be treated. We're all here to help each other become more productive. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to HyperPlanner and productivity. Off-topic posts should go in the General Discussion category.

Search First

Before posting a question, search the forum to see if it's been answered. This helps reduce duplicate threads and gets you answers faster.

Share Knowledge

If you've solved a problem, share your solution! Your answer might help someone else facing the same issue.

No Self-Promotion

Don't spam the forum with promotional content. Sharing relevant tools is fine, but excessive self-promotion will be removed.
