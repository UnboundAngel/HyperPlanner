Keyboard Shortcuts

Master HyperPlanner with these keyboard shortcuts. Work faster, navigate smarter, and keep your hands on the keyboard.

Platform-Specific Keys

Throughout this guide, we show shortcuts for both Mac and Windows/Linux. Cmd on Mac corresponds to Ctrl on Windows/Linux, and Option corresponds to Alt.

Customizing Shortcuts

HyperPlanner allows you to customize keyboard shortcuts to match your workflow.

  1. Open Settings

    Press Cmd/Ctrl + , to open settings

  2. Navigate to Keyboard

    Select "Keyboard Shortcuts" from the sidebar

  3. Find & Edit

    Search for the action you want to customize, click on it, and press your new key combination

  4. Resolve Conflicts

    If your new shortcut conflicts with an existing one, you'll be prompted to reassign or clear the conflict

Shortcut Conflicts

Some shortcuts may conflict with your browser or operating system. If a shortcut doesn't work, check your system preferences or try an alternative combination.
