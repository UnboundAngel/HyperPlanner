How HyperPlanner Works

Deep dive into the details that make it yours

01

Capture Everything, Naturally

One-Line Parsing

Type a single line and HyperPlanner extracts:

"team sync tomorrow 10am @work !high #planning"
  • Date/Time: "tomorrow 10am" ‚Üí parsed to exact datetime
  • Context: @work ‚Üí auto-tagged with work context
  • Priority: !high ‚Üí flagged as high priority
  • Tags: #planning ‚Üí categorized

Custom Phrase Mapping

Teach HyperPlanner your language:

  • "eod" ‚Üí due today at 5pm
  • "next sprint" ‚Üí due in 2 weeks
  • "urgent" ‚Üí !high + @inbox

Inline Subtasks

Break down tasks as you type:

"Launch campaign > draft copy > design assets > schedule posts"

Smart Conflict Hints

If you schedule over existing events, you'll see instant visual warnings. Decide whether to move, overlap, or reschedule.

02

Unified Tasks & Events

Everything in One Model

No more "is this a task or event?" Every item has:

  • Title & Description: Rich text, markdown support
  • Context & Priority: Work, personal, urgent, low
  • Timing: Due date, start/end times, recurrence rules
  • Metadata: Tags, location, status, subtasks

Drag Across Views

Grab a task from your Kanban board and drop it onto the calendar‚Äîit instantly becomes time-blocked. Drag an event to "In Progress" and it moves to your active list.

Conflict Highlighting

Overlapping events glow with a warning border. Double-booked? You'll know immediately, with suggestions to resolve.

Recurrence Logic

Daily, weekly, monthly, custom intervals. Skip instances, edit future occurrences, or modify the whole series.

03

Layout & View Control

Flexible Panel System

Every panel is draggable and resizable. Arrange your workspace exactly how you need it:

  • Calendar View: Day/week/month with density controls
  • Kanban Lanes: Inbox, In Progress, Done, Archived
  • Focus Duo: Split view with today + backlog
  • Hero Summary: Stats, focus load, next actions

Density Controls

Calendar time slots: 60-minute, 30-minute, or 15-minute intervals. Perfect for time-blocking at any granularity.

Component Variants

Every task/event card has display modes:

  • Minimal: Title only, maximum density
  • Compact: Title + key metadata
  • Detailed: Full description, tags, subtasks
  • Wide: Expanded for focus work

Device Presets

Save different layouts per device. Desktop gets a three-panel split; mobile switches to single-column focus mode. HyperPlanner remembers.

Design Tokens

Fine-tune spacing, radius, shadows:

  • Density: Compact, comfortable, spacious
  • Radius: Sharp, rounded, pill-shaped
  • Shadows: Flat, subtle, elevated

Full Token Control

Change every variable in the Theme Lab:

  • Accents: Primary, secondary, tertiary colors
  • Glass Strength: Opacity, blur, tint
  • Borders: Width, color, style
  • Backgrounds: Solid, gradient, mesh
  • Motion: Animation speed, easing curves

Light/Dark Morphing

Smooth transitions between modes. Auto-switch based on time of day or manual toggle. Every theme adapts.

Icon Packs

Semantic icons for contexts: briefcase for @work, home for @personal. Override any icon with your own SVG or emoji.

Contrast Guardrails

Text and inputs auto-adjust to maintain WCAG AA contrast. You stay creative; HyperPlanner keeps it readable.

Export & Share

Save your theme as JSON. Share with friends or the community. Import themes from others and remix them.
